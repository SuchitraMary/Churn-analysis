## 📊 Churn Analysis Using Machine Learning
# 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in industries like banking, telecom, and subscription-based services. Retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones.

This project focuses on predicting customer churn using Machine Learning techniques and identifying the key factors that influence customer attrition.

The goal is to help businesses take proactive retention actions through predictive analytics.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

Identify customers who are most likely to churn (leave the service).

Understand the major factors influencing customer churn.

Build a predictive model to classify customers as Churn or Non-Churn.

Support data-driven decision-making for customer retention strategies.

Churn refers to the process where a customer stops doing business with a company.

---

# 💼 Business Value

Customer retention plays a crucial role in long-term profitability.

By predicting churn, businesses can:

- 🎯 Offer personalized retention strategies

- 💰 Reduce customer acquisition costs

- 📈 Improve customer lifetime value (CLV)

- 🤝 Increase customer satisfaction

- 📊 Make strategic business decisions using data

Proactively identifying at-risk customers enables companies to implement targeted campaigns and improve overall revenue.

# 📊 Key Insights

- Customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn rates.

- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn probability.

- Customers using electronic check payment methods tend to churn more.

- Long-tenure customers are less likely to churn.
